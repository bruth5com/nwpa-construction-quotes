Kitchen & Bathroom Remodels Built for How You Live

Kitchens and bathrooms take more daily wear than any other room in the house. When you're ready for a remodel, we connect you with local contractors around Butler, Venango, Crawford and Mercer Counties who handle everything from a single-fixture update to a full gut renovation.

Whether you're modernizing a dated kitchen, updating a bathroom for aging in place, or reworking a layout that's never quite worked for your family, the contractors in our network plan the job right before any demo starts.

What's Included

  • Full kitchen remodels — cabinets, countertops, layout changes, appliances
  • Bathroom renovations — tile, vanities, walk-in showers, tub replacements
  • Aging-in-place updates — grab bars, walk-in tubs, wider doorways
  • Plumbing and electrical rework tied to the remodel

Common Questions

How long does a kitchen remodel usually take?

A full kitchen remodel typically runs 3-6 weeks depending on scope; a bathroom renovation is often 1-3 weeks. Your contractor will give you a firm timeline during the estimate.

Do I need to move out during the work?

Most homeowners stay in the house during a kitchen or bathroom remodel, though you'll want a backup plan for that room while it's out of commission.
