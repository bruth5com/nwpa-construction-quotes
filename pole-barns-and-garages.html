Common Questions

Do I need a permit for a pole barn?

Most townships in NW Pennsylvania require a permit for structures over a certain size — your contractor can tell you exactly what applies to your property and handle the paperwork.

How long does a pole barn take to build?

A standard pole barn is typically framed and enclosed in 1-3 weeks after the site is prepped, weather permitting.
