Common Questions

Should I repair my deck or replace it?

If the frame and footings are sound but the boards are worn, repair often makes sense. If the structure itself is failing, replacement is usually the safer and more cost-effective route — your contractor will tell you honestly which situation you're in.

How long does building a new deck take?

Most standard decks are built in 1-2 weeks once materials are on site; covered porches with a roof structure can take longer.
