Homeowners across Cranberry, Wexford, and Butler County ask the same question every week: How much does drywall repair or installation cost near me?

Whether it's a small hole, water damage, or a full basement remodel, drywall pricing depends on the size of the job and the finish level you want. Here's what homeowners in Western PA typically pay in 2026.

Drywall Installation Costs

For basements, additions, and full remodels:

Job TypeTypical Cost
Per square foot installed$1.50–$3.50

Pricing depends on number of rooms, ceiling height, framing layout, and finish level — smooth, orange peel, or knockdown texture.

What Affects Drywall Pricing?

  • Material costs — sheetrock, joint compound, tape
  • Labor time and crew size
  • Ceiling height — anything over 9 feet costs more
  • Number of corners and angles
  • Texture finish level
  • Whether painting is included or separate

When Should You Call a Drywall Contractor?

  • Water stains or soft spots in walls or ceilings
  • Cracks spreading across seams
  • Holes from doorknobs, furniture, or accidents
  • Remodeling or finishing a basement
  • Uneven or wavy walls after settling
